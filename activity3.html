Tech Giants: Apple Inc.

Introduction

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company.
It is best known for the iPhone, iPad, and Mac computers.

The Founders

The company was founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne.
It was started in a garage in April 1976.

Interesting Fact

Did you know? Apple's first logo featured Issac Newton sitting under an apple tree.
Today, it is one of the most valuable companies in the world.
